POLISCI 148 Chinese Politics The Transformation and the Era of Reform POLISCI 348 Overview of the reforms in China since 1978 that have made its economy one of the fastest growing in the world yet it still has the Chinese Communist Party at the helm wielding one party rule Key questions addressed include the following What has been the process and challenges of reform that have reshaped economic landscape What are the political consequences of these dramatic economic changes Why has the CCP remained strong while other communist regimes have failed Markets have spread but what is the role of the state What are the opportunities for political participation and prospects for political change Materials will include readings lectures and selected films This course has no prerequisites
