Media Advisory Carnegie Mellon Information Networking Institute Hosts Lean In A Perspective From Women In Technology Panel Contact Sherry Stokes stokes Event Lean In A Perspective from Women in Technology is the title of a panel discussion sponsored by Carnegie Mellon University Information Networking Institute INI and Women at INI WINI student organization INI Director Dena Haritos Tsamitis will moderate a panel of distinguished executives in information security privacy and risk management who will share their personal and professional perspectives on their ascent to a role Attendees will have a rare opportunity to interact with these leaders and engage in the conversation Panelists include Joyce Brocaglia chief executive officer at Alta Associates and founder of the Executive Women Forum Michelle Dennedy chief privacy officer at McAfee and Holly Ridgeway chief information security officer at PNC Financial Services Group The event which is free and open to the CMU community is inspired by Lean In Women Work and the Will to Lead a bestseller by Facebook Chief Operating Officer Sheryl Sandberg Registration for this event is recommended but not required Register online at
