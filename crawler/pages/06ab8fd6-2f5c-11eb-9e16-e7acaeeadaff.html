InclusionInternship OpportunitiesWorking at the SEI SEI Education and Outreach License SEI Materials Insider Threat Partner Opportunity Become an SEI Partner for Insider Threat As an SEI Partner for Insider Threat your organization can license the CERT Insider Threat Vulnerability Assessment tool and sponsor Insider Threat Vulnerability Assessors ITVA to perform assessments The Insider Threat Vulnerability Assessment helps organizations to identify issues impacting their insider threat risk design and implement tactical countermeasures formulate a strategic action plan for risk mitigation Benefits Becoming an SEI partner for insider threat is a way to be recognized as a leader in the insider threat space For federal contractors this partnership is a way to get the tools they need to implement insider threat programs in compliance with Executive Order 13587 which is required by the latest update to NISPOM Assessments conducted by individuals with the Insider Threat Vulnerability Assessor ITVA credential can help organizations detect and respond to organizational vulnerabilities using processes based on actual cases and decades of research by the CERT Insider Threat Center in partnership with the Department of Defense the Department of Homeland Security the Secret Service other federal agencies the intelligence community private industry academia and the vendor community Evaluations conducted by individuals with the Insider Threat Program Evaluator ITPE credential can help organizations gain a better understanding of the effectiveness of their established insider threat programs using the CERT Insider Threat Program Evaluation methodology Learn More For more information about the benefits and process of becoming a partner see Licensing SEI Materials Contact
