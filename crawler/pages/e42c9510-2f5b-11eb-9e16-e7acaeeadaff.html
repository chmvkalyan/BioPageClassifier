Collaboration Web Conferencing Zoom How To Use Zoom Manage Zoom Interviews Manage Zoom Interviews Zoom allows you to easily set up and manage interviews using the Breakout Rooms feature With Breakout Rooms you can split a single Zoom meeting in up to 50 separate sessions You can then arrange for a candidate to meet with several different interviewers in separate Breakout Rooms Expectations for the Host The interview host should be available for the entire day to answer questions and facilitate interview transitions The interview host will need to have the Zoom meeting open throughout the day The host will be unable to host other Zoom meetings during the time the interview is taking place Resources Manage Zoom Interviews pdf Before You Start Make sure the Zoom application is updated Open the Zoom app and click the menu upper left Click Check for Updates If an update is available click Update Gather the following The interview schedule which defines interview blocks times and interviewers during the session The contact information for the interview candidate The contact information for all interviewers CMU employees Schedule the Zoom Interview Schedule the meeting for the interview candidate in Zoom Schedule and send invitations to your interviewers by copying the Zoom invitation to your application Exchange or Google Mail Send a Meeting Request to each interviewer for their assigned time block Review Manage Zoom Interviews pdf and Breakout Rooms for Interviews video Practice using Breakout Rooms with your coworkers before your interview meeting Day of the Interview On the day of the interview start the Zoom meeting and set up the Breakout Rooms Facilitate the interview meeting process using the following features Assign participants to the appropriate Breakout Rooms according to the interview schedule Join each Breakout Room as the host at the start of each interview block to facilitate the interview process Leave each Breakout Room as the host once each
