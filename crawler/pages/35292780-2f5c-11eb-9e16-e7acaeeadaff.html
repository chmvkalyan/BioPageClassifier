page ILAC 122A Radical Poetry The in Latin America and Spain The first few decades of the 20th century ushered in a dynamic literary and aesthetic renewal in Spain and Latin America Young poets sought a radical change in response to a rapidly changing world one marked by the horrors of World War I and the rise of a new technological urban society This course will focus on the poetry and attendant manifestos of movements such as Creacionismo Ultraismo Estridentismo Surrealismo and other How did the European Futurism Dada and Surrealism inform such aesthetic turns In what ways did poetry assimilate modern visual culture while questioning established poetics Authors may include Aleixandre Borges Diego de Torre Huidobro Larrea Lorca Maples Arce Neruda Tablada and Vallejo Taught in
