University Athletics TartanProud tartanathletics Carnegie Mellon University Athletics Update Spring Sports Cancelled Posted Mar 13 2020 PITTSBURGH As the outbreak of the coronavirus disease continues to develop Carnegie Mellon University and the Department of Athletics have developed the following policies to help ensure the health and of our constituents Intercollegiate Athletics Effective immediately all spring intercollegiate competition and activities are canceled for the spring 2020 season Per the National Collegiate Athletic Association NCAA statement released March 12 2020 NCAA Winter and Spring Championships have been cancelled Per the University Athletic Association UAA statement released March 13 2020 All UAA Spring 2020 Regular Season and Championship Competition have been cancelled Leadership programs and the Advisory Council SAAC Field Day have been cancelled Facilities Recreation and Activities Skibo Gym and Wiegand Gym will be closed for recreation and equipment will not be available for Access to the spaces will be restricted The Cohon University Center and Tepper School fitness centers as well as the Cohon University Center pool will be closed until further notice The varsity weight room will also be closed This follows guidance from the Allegheny County Health Department In person Group X and personal training are suspended until further notice Virtual offerings are available Intramural programming and club sport activities are cancelled for spring 2020 Kiltie Band rehearsals and performances have been suspended For coronavirus information from the university please visit Carnegie Mellon s Coronavirus alert webpage Facebook Twitter Email Print Addthis Carnegie Mellon UniversityOfficial Site of Carnegie Mellon AthleticsSkibo
