Times printer friendly page POLISCI 120C American Political Institutions in Uncertain Times PUBLPOL 124 This course examines how the rules that govern elections and the policy process determine political outcomes It explores the historical forces that have shaped American political institutions contemporary challenges to governing and prospects for change Topics covered include partisan polarization and legislative gridlock the politicization of the courts electoral institutions and voting rights the expansion of presidential power campaign finance and lobbying representational biases among elected officials and the role of political institutions in maintaining the rule of law Throughout emphasis will be placed on the strategic interactions between Congress the presidency
