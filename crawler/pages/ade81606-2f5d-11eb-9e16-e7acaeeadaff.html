implications far beyond the linear sequence of the DNA We will consider two questions related to the genome coupled with examples from consequences Firstly what does the genome say about our past where we came from and how we might fit into the tapestry of the human race We will look at examples from history and anonymized patients to highlight the consequences of these question for people Next we will consider what the genome tells us about the future how might it foretell our individual future and how might this be translated into patient treatment We will examine the promises
