implies for controlling security events and incidents Publisher CERT Subjects Incident ManagementIncident Management Abstract An incident management capability is the ability to provide management of computer security events and incidents It implies management for controlling or directing how security events and incidents should be handled This involves defining a process to follow with supporting policies and procedures in place assigning roles and responsibilities having appropriate equipment infrastructure tools and supporting materials ready and having qualified staff identified and trained
