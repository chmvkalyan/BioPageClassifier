Computing Best Practices in a Dynamic Industry September 2019 Presentation Nathaniel Richmond This talk focuses on best practices developed by the SEI in recent years It addresses how to translate practices into detailed plans that you can implement and how to adapt those practices to changes in cloud computing Publisher Software Engineering Institute Subjects Cloud ComputingCloud Computing Abstract Information technology and security have always had to adapt to advances and changes in technology The cloud computing environment has been particularly volatile subject to changes in services the introduction of new services and the elimination of services Although some best practices for cloud computing are sound and likely to endure organizations that want to use them must transform those practices into a detailed plan that they can apply and they must update them to reduce risk and ensure proper evaluation of services and vendors Share Download PDF Part of a Collection
