the SEI Boleng joined the SEI in 2012 after 21 years of service as an officer in the Air Force During his service he was a member of the computer science faculty at the Air Force Academy for eight years and was honored with the Outstanding Academy Educator in Computer Science award for academic year He has operational Air Force experience as a network engineer with the 1st Combat Communications Squadron where he deployed in support of the Bosnian War leading an intelligence software development team in Air Forces Europe the interoperability efforts for Forces Korea and the integration efforts in Air Force Space Command Additionally he served as a flight commander and chief of maintenance in the 21st Space Communications Squadron and commanded the 21st Mission Support Squadron on Peterson Air Force Base Colorado In 2010 he deployed to Kabul Afghanistan in support of Operation Enduring Freedom as a mentor to the Computer Science Department head at the National Military Academy of Afghanistan and as a member of the International Security Assistance Force ISAF Boleng earned PhD and master s degrees in mathematical and computer sciences from the Colorado School of Mines and a bachelor s degree in computer science from the Air Force Academy He is a senior member of both the Association for Computing Machinery ACM and the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers
