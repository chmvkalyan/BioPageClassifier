Petersburg FL Publisher Software Engineering Institute Abstract Over the past few years Statoil has explored how designs platforms and capabilities can be used to improve core upstream oil and gas business processes Our hypothesis is that properties such as collaboration and negotiation can be used to improve human decision making and thereby enhance both operational efficiency and safety To most of us an autonomous system in line with Stanley Kubrick s HAL 9000 from 2001 A Space Odyssey is unacceptable Therefore an overarching principle in an architecture is that the human operator must be in control and that the system should be regarded as a useful servant To support this we have developed an architecture whose bearing concepts are variable and delegated autonomy In this presentation we will discuss the principles experiences and challenges encountered with this architecture Share Download PDF Part of a Collection SATURN 2012 Presentations Ask a question about this
