Secure DevelopmentSecure Development Listen Watch Abstract The Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency DARPA and other agencies are expressing significant interest in blockchain technology because it promises inherent transparency resiliency and nonrepudiation which can be used to protect sensitive infrastructure At the same time numerous incidents of blockchain coding errors that cause major damage to organizations have raised serious concerns about blockchain adoption In this podcast Eliezer Kanal and Michael Coblenz discuss the creation of Obsidian a novel programming language
