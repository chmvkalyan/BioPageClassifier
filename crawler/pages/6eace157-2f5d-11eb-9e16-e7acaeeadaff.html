excludable from American society These dynamics mirror the historical forms of exclusion imposed on immigrants from places as diverse as China Eastern Europe Ireland Italy Japan and much of Africa These groups varied experiences of exclusion underscore the encoding of race into immigration policy and practice Readings and discussions center on how immigration law has become racialized in its
