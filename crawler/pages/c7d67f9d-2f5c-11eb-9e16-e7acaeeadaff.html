medical care and social services available to all patients regardless of insurance status ability to pay or even complaint Emergency Departments ED are safety nets for local communities EDs serve as a window into society and offer opportunities for intervention The field of Social Emergency Medicine uses this unique position to investigate societal patterns of health inequity and develop solutions to decrease health disparities for vulnerable populations This dinner seminar will explore psychosocial economic and medical factore that contribute to human health from the perspective of ED providers Each session will cover a different topic of societal emergency medicine such as opioid use human trafficking firearms and homelessness Possible interventions will also be discussed
