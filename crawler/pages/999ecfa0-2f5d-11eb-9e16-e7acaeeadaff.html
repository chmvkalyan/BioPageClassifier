Do you have a duty to vote Should immigrants be allowed to vote Should we make voting mandatory How if at all should we regulate campaign finance Should we even have elections at all In this course we will explore these and other ethical questions related to electoral participation and the design of electoral institutions We will evaluate arguments from political philosophers political scientists and politicians to better understand how electoral systems promote important democratic values and how this affects citizens and political leaders ethical obligations We will focus in particular on issues in electoral design that have been relevant in recent US elections gerrymandering though many of the ethical issues we will discuss in this course will be relevant in any electoral democracy
