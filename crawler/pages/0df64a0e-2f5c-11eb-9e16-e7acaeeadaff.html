execution and sustainment Publisher Software Engineering Institute Report Number DOI Digital Object Identifier Subjects Acquisition SupportAcquisition Support Abstract Intellectual property IP is a critical consideration of most acquisitions that require sophisticated items or components and performance Preparation of an intellectual property strategy is now a required element in Department of Defense acquisition guidance and major efforts such as Better Buying Power The IP Strategy serves as a plan for competitive and affordable acquisition and sustainment of license rights in IP over the entire item or component lifecycle This report is intended to help program managers understand categories of IP various IP challenges and approaches to assessing the license rights that the program needs for execution and sustainment Developing a strategy for the entire product lifecycle to obtain specific rights can be a major challenge The program manager must now prepare the IP Strategy early in the development lifecycle prior to release of the solicitation and update it prior to each milestone Share Download PDF Ask a question about this Special Report Report a Vulnerability to Subscribe
