All Greek Community Standards Board AGCSB All Greek Community Standards Board AGCSB The Carnegie Mellon University All Greek Community Standards Board AGCSB serves to enforce the standards rules and regulations set forth in the Panhellenic Council PHC Interfraternity Council IFC and Multicultural Greek Council MGC communities The goal is to encourage the responsible decision making and behavior of chapters and colonies in the fraternity and sorority community and to outline the process of response when their decisions and behaviors do not meet the standards we have set for the Greek community The community standards board protocol serves to promote accountability and encourage shared governance of organizations within the Carnegie Mellon University community To learn more please download the AGCSB Policies and Procedures Reports of chapter misconduct can be made by any member of the CMU community If you are aware of a chapter in violation of a council University policy please submit an Incident Report Form For any questions about the AGCSB or chapter misconduct please contact Jesse Koch Associate Director of Greek Life Division of Student Affairs Athletics Physical
