help others see details of the implementation Abstract Formative assessments allow learners to quickly identify knowledge gaps In traditional educational settings expert instructors can create assessments but in informal learning environments it is difficult for novice learners to self assess because they don t know what they don t know This paper introduces Questimator an automated system that generates assessment questions for any topic contained within Wikipedia Given a topic Questimator traverses the Wikipedia graph to find and rank related topics and uses article text to form questions answers and distractor options In a study with 833 participants from Mechanical Turk we found that participants scores on quizzes correlated well with their scores on existing online quizzes on topics ranging from philosophy to economics Also Questimator generates questions with comparable discriminatory power as existing online quizzes Our results suggest Questimator may be useful for
