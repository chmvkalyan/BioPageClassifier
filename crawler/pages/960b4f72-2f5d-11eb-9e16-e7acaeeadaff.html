that someone would kill you was more than 1 in 10 but if you were born in the twentieth century AD it was more like 1 in 100 despite that world wars genocides and nuclear weapons In the 2010s it is just 1 in 150 This course tries to explain this astonishing shift away from violence We will look at the history of war from the Stone Age to the robot age including the conflicts of the 2010s and we will draw on everything from anthropology and archaeology to biology and psychology as we try to answer one of the biggest questions of all will there ever be a world without war Students learn how to
