Division University Audit Services Internal Audit Review Areas Review Areas Areas are selected for internal audit based upon an assessment of risk at the institutional level A risk is any event or action that adversely impacts the university s ability to achieve its objectives Risks can impact organizations on varying scales of magnitude and likelihood Areas with a high degree of risk likelihood or impact are often subject to more frequent audit Some audits are required by governmental agencies or other outside entities such as the university s external auditors and may be performed annually Other audits are discretionary and involve departmental operational or process reviews These reviews are scheduled in consultation with management Internal audits are prioritized
