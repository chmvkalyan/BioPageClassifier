Science HDHDQM Overview The Bachelor of Science Degree in Human Development HD is designed to support student learning about the mechanisms of growth and change across the lifespan With areas of focus in developmental science educational psychology and statistical methodology HD majors will explore the biological social emotional and cognitive processes of learning and development from conception
