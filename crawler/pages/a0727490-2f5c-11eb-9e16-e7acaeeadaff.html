apply these skills to both works of art and medical cases Significant focus will be on engaging in group discussions where they will collaboratively use visual evidence to generate and defend hypothesis Drawing and sketching from life will play a critical role in honing observational skills through weekly assignments workshops and a final project The interactive nature of this course
