Resilience Management Model RMM Resilience Management Model RMM Abstract CERT Resilience Management Model is an innovative and transformative way to manage operational resilience in complex environments distills years of research into best practices for managing the security and survivability of people information technology and facilities It integrates these best practices into a unified maturity model that encompasses security business continuity and IT operations By using organizations can escape approaches to managing operational risk and align to achieve strategic resilience management goals This book both introduces and presents the model in its entirety It begins with essential background for all professionals whether they have previously used process improvement models or not Next it explains Generic Goals and Practices and discusses various approaches for using the model Short essays by a number of contributors illustrate how can be applied for different purposes or can be used to improve an existing program Finally the book provides a complete baseline understanding of all 26 process areas included in This book will be valuable to anyone seeking to improve the mission assurance of services including leaders of large enterprise or organizational units security or business continuity specialists managers of large IT operations and those
