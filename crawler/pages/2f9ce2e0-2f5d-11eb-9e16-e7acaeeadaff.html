Over the last decade tech companies have invested in shaping user behavior sometimes for altruistic reasons like helping people change bad habits into good ones and sometimes for financial reasons such as increasing engagement In this course students explore the design of systems information and interface for human use We will model the flow of interactions data and context and crafting a design that is useful appropriate and robust Students will design and prototype utility apps or games as a response to the challenges presented We will also examine the ethical consequences of design
