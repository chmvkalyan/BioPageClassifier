Publisher Software Engineering Institute Abstract The Ninth International Workshop on Managing Technical Debt was collocated with the 18th International Conference on Agile Software Development XP 2017 in Cologne The technical debt research community continues to expand through collaborations of industry tool vendors and academia The main topic of this year workshop was on the impact of agile development approaches on the management of technical debt The workshop summary was published as Technical Debt in Agile Development Report on the Ninth Workshop on Managing Technical Debt MTD 2017
