the challenges facing the university The Finance Division exists to Act as Stewards of the financial resources entrusted to the university in support of its vision to have a transformative impact on society through continual innovation in education research creativity and entrepreneurship 1 Serve and Support students faculty staff and others who have a vested interest in the financial activity
